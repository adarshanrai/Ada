The Untouched Bajrey

Mountains are the beginning and the end of all natural scenery. - John Ruskin

Bajrey offers a breathtaking vantage point, where you can gaze upon the majestic Kanchenjunga, its snow-capped peaks rising proudly against the sky. The cool, crisp air and the rugged, scenic terrain of Bajrey, with vast open spaces, add to the tranquil beauty of the place.
